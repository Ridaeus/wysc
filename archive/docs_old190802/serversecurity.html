  1. Inauthentic Users

Inauthentic Users

Inauthentic Users are defined as any user of Wysc that intends to adversely affect Wysc, its platform, and/or its users for personal gain through means that do not align with Wysc's goals.

Quarantining Users

Tree rank users may use the command d.nsfw @user to quarantine a misbehaving user from the server's main channels by @mentioning the user.
