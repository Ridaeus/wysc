How Wysc Works

Wysc is primarily a virtual interactive study space offering social and music amenities.

Our main channel is #lounge, where all our members can mingle and interact.

All study conversations take place in #study-center. This is where Accountability, Pomodoro/Group Study, and other general-school related topics are discussed. In addition, we also offer subject-specific discussion channels to help others discuss homework topics without worrying about being flooded.

Wysc Cafe offers many social and musical services, including #socialgram and #lofi-vaporwave. If you are a music creator, check out our Music Creators section.
